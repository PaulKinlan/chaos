# Dashboard

Your home screen for CHAOS -- a quick overview of what your agents are doing and what needs attention.

## What This View Does

The Dashboard gives you a birds-eye view of your agent system. It surfaces recent activity, pinned artifacts, and suggestions so you can quickly see what happened and what to do next.

## Activity Feed

- Shows recent actions across all agents -- completed tasks, new artifacts, channel messages
- Each activity item links to the relevant view for more detail
- The feed updates in real time as agents work

## Pinned Artifacts

- Pin important artifacts from the Artifacts view and they appear here
- Quick access to dashboards, reports, or pages your agents have built
- Click a pinned artifact to view it in full

## Suggestions

- CHAOS suggests actions based on your current setup and activity
- Suggestions might include setting up a new channel, creating a hook, or trying a feature
- Dismiss suggestions you are not interested in

## Agent Status

- See which agents are active, idle, or working on tasks
- Quick indicator of how many jobs are in progress
- Click an agent name to jump to its settings or chat

## Tips

- Check the dashboard at the start of a session to see what happened while you were away
- Pin artifacts you reference often so they are one click away
- Use suggestions as a guide for getting more out of CHAOS
- The dashboard is a read-only overview -- use the sidebar to navigate to specific views for actions
